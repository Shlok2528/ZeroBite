Our Mission

At ZeroBite, our mission is to eradicate hunger by providing nutritious meals to those in need. We believe food is a basic human right, and we work towards a future where everyone has enough to eat each day.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a stronger and healthier Bharat where all children have access to wholesome food. We believe that by providing nutritious meals, we can enable children to develop, learn, and flourish, preventing hunger from hindering their potential. Our ultimate goal is to pave the way for a better and financially secure future for everyone in the community.

What Sets Us Apart

Comprehensive Care

We believe in a holistic approach that goes beyond just serving meals. Ensuring consistent nutrition for school children helps boost their attendance. This enhancement in their learning experiences paves the way for brighter futures.
