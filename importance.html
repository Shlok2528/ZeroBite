"Leftovers Are Future Meals"

Food waste is not just about losing food—it’s about wasting the water, energy, and effort that went into growing, transporting, and preparing it. By managing food waste properly, we can cut down pollution, save money, and ensure that more food reaches people who need it. Effective food waste management is not just an environmental responsibility; it is a social and economic necessity for a sustainable future.Remember, every grain saved is a step toward a greener planet and a kinder world.
