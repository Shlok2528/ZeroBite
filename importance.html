"Give It Away instead of Wasting It"

Food Waste is a growing global challenge that affects our environment, economy, and communities. We aim to promote smarter consumption, responsible disposal, and sustainable paractices. By connecting individuals, businesses, and organisations, we encourage collaborative action toward a cleaner, greener future.

About Us

ZeroBite, founded in November 2025 under Shlok Deepak Bhanushali, Mahek Gitesh Bhanushali and Parag Nilesh Bhaidkar, is dedicated to eradicating hunger by providing nutritious meals to those in need. We believe access to food is a basic human right and envision a healthier Bharat where every child can flourish. Focused on educating people on how to manage food waste, collecting excess food from events and delivering it to vulnerable communities.
